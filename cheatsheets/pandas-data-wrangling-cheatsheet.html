Performance & Best Practices

‚ö° Performance Tips

  • ‚Ä¢ Prefer Parquet over CSV for I/O
  • ‚Ä¢ Use nullable dtypes (Int64, string, boolean)
  • ‚Ä¢ Downcast numerics with pd.to_numeric(..., downcast="integer")
  • ‚Ä¢ Convert high-cardinality strings to categorical
  • ‚Ä¢ Avoid apply(axis=1)
  • ‚Ä¢ Chain with .assign + .pipe
  • ‚Ä¢ Use query/eval for large filters
  • ‚Ä¢ For big data: chunked reading or Dask/Polars

‚ö†Ô∏è Anti-patterns & Gotchas

  • ‚Ä¢ Chained indexing = bugs:
    Bad: df[df.a>0]["b"] = 1
    Good: df.loc[df.a>0, "b"] = 1
  • ‚Ä¢ inplace=True rarely helps
  • ‚Ä¢ Mixing NaN and strings ‚Üí cast to "string"
  • ‚Ä¢ GroupBy drops NAs unless dropna=False

üîó Method-Chaining Pattern

tidy = ( pd.read_parquet("sales.parquet") .convert_dtypes() .assign(ts=lambda d: pd.to_datetime(d["ts"], utc=True), net=lambda d: d["amount"] - d["tax"]) .query("net >= 0 and status == 'OK'") .pipe(lambda d: d.assign( month=d["ts"].dt.to_period("M"), seg=pd.cut(d["net"], [-np.inf,50,200,np.inf], labels=["low","mid","high"]) )) .groupby(["month","seg"], dropna=False) .agg(n=("id","count"), revenue=("net","sum")) .reset_index() .sort_values(["month","seg"]) )
